A Journey Through Time

1963

McLaren was founded by Bruce McLaren, a visionary New Zealand race car driver, engineer, and inventor.

1966

McLaren entered Formula 1, quickly making a name for itself with innovation and competitive performance on the track.

1990s

The McLaren F1, a groundbreaking supercar, set new standards for speed, design, and engineering excellence.

Present

Today, McLaren continues to lead in automotive performance, fusing cutting-edge technology with stunning design in every road and track car.
